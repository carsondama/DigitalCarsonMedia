About Me

As shown above, I'm on the left and my photographer buddy Matt is on the right!

My name is Carson and I'm an Information Techology Major at KSU. I'm a full time student and a hobbyist Motorsport Photographer. I started over 5 years ago when I saw a Toyota Supra during my lunchbreak in highschool and have not stopped since. I've travelled thousands of miles and spent at least 50 hours shooting at racetracks on the east coast! I have a passion for motorsports and cars in general! When I'm not studying or taking photos I'm probably spending hours perfecting the editing of the aformentioned photos! It is my passion and a hobby that brings me joy!
